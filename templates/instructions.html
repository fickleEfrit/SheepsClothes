How To Play

General Rules

Among sheep, there is a wolf out for blood.

Against one dedicated 'wolf', 'sheep' players are given a codeword. Their goal is to smoke out the wolf, by saying related words.

The wolf, though, has an inkling of what the word might be. If they figure out the exact word, they blend in and the sheep are doomed!

Starting Up

All players should start on the main page.

When everyone is there, the game can begin by hitting the start button.

Players are then assigned to be the Wolf or one of many Sheep.

Sheep Players

If the following page shows that you were made a sheep, it will also show the "Target Word."

You and the other sheep are trying to figure out who the wolf is by saying words related to the Target Word.

Players who have the word should be able to describe it easily; don't make it too obvious what the word is though!

Wolf Player

If the following page shows that you were made the wolf, it will also show a "Hint Word."

Your goal is to blend in with the sheep, by guessing the target word using the related words the sheep say.

The Hint Word is useful when the Wolf is the first one to go, and acts as the "Sheep's Clothes."

Win Condition

Any player, on their turn, may accuse another player of being the Wolf.

A vote then occurs; if the majority of players agree, the accusation goes through.

The accused may not vote. If it is a tie or more than half the players disagree, the game continues.

If the accusation is correct, the sheep win. If the accusation is incorrect, the wolf wins.

The wolf may also, on their turn, admit they are the wolf and guess the Target Word.

If they get the Target Word correct, the wolf wins, but if they get it wrong, the sheep wins.
